Cranberry Salad

Description

This is a disgusting recipe for a cranberry "salad" that only an insane person would eat. He would eat it with great joy as it might be his last meal. Featuring delicious ingridients like sugar, gelatin and celery this dish is sure to change your perception on what food can be.

A fabulous blend of cranberries, nuts, and fruit! Cranberry or Cherry jello may be used instead of raspberry.
