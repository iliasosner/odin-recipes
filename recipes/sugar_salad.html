Sugar Salad

Description

This is a delicious recipe for sugar salad that only a sane person would eat. He would eat it with great joy as it might be his first meal. Featuring delicious ingridients like Sweet and sour things, this dish is sure to change your perception on what food can be.

Ingredients

  • 0.25 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 head green leaf lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk the vegetable oil, vinegar, and sugar in a small bowl. Place the lettuce in a large bowl. Drizzle the oil mixture over the lettuce and toss to coat.
