Cookie Salad

Description

This is a crumbly recipe for a cookie salad that only a dog would eat. He would eat it with great joy as it might be his second meal of the day. Featuring delicious ingridients like cookies ,this dish is sure to change your digestive tract

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fudge stripe cookies
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 bananas, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip), thawed
  • 1 (8 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained

Steps

  1. Break 2/3 of the fudge stripe cookies into quarters.
  2. Beat buttermilk and pudding mix together in a large bowl until slightly thickened. Stir quartered cookies into pudding. Fold bananas, whipped topping, and oranges into pudding mixture. Top with remaining whole cookies.
